Mamma Lisa's Pizzeria

Mamma Lisa's Pizzeria does a solid slice, but where they really shine is with their Eggplant Parm Sub. It's so so good. Also it's a pretty chill place to sit. They've either got Pawn Stars or Leave It to Beaver on the TV. Very chill spot to stop in for some lunch after a long day of web programming... in fact...




My favorite food is eggplant parmasean. It's just about the only dish I'll go to the trouble of deep-frying for. The trick is to sweat the sliced eggplant for at least 30 min prior to battering to remove the more bitter compounds, then pat dry before coating with seasoned flour, dredging in beaten egg, and covering with breadcrumbs (I prefer panko). Then deep fry until golden brown. Then we place that on a thin layer of tomato sauce, top with a bit of low moisture mozzerella and blast it under the broiler for a few couple minutes and serve with pasta of choice. Delicious... can you tell that I like eggplant parm?
